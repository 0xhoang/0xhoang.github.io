Embarking on a Professional Journey at Tiki

Introduction

Joining the dynamic world of Tiki, a thriving marketplace for eCommerce brands and sellers, marked a significant step in my professional journey.

Website: https://www.tiki.vn

My Role and Contributions

During my tenure at Tiki, I had the opportunity to work on various projects. Here are some of the key projects I was involved in:

Project: Talaria - Platform for Order, Inventory, Shipping, Fulfillment, Checkout

  • Built comprehensive function tests for Talaria, ensuring its reliability.
  • Developed API RESTful services for Talaria, utilizing PHP, Laravel, and Falcon.
    • Technology used: Laravel
  • Implemented payment gateways such as Cybersource, 123pay, Momo, Zalopay, and Onepay, creating efficient and secure payment processing solutions.
  • Maintained and enhanced the core structure of Talaria to keep the system up-to-date and efficient.
  • Provided technical support to address issues and bugs for other teams and customers.
  • Applied Event Sourcing to handle producer/consumer events, particularly in the context of order creation, using technologies like RabbitMQ and Kafka.
    • Technology used: Kafka

Project: Marketplace Seller Success

  • Led the development of projects like OutStock Notification and Revenue Report, using a combination of Java and Golang.
    • Technology used: Golang

My journey at Tiki allowed me to work on diverse and challenging projects, contributing to the success of this startup ecosystem.
