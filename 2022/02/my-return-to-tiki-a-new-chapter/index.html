My Return to Tiki: A New Chapter

Introduction

In early 2022, I made the exciting decision to return to Tiki, a company close to my heart. The driving force behind this decision was the exceptional team and the company’s inspiring mission.

What I’ve Been Up To

Upon my return to Tiki, I embarked on several remarkable projects, making a significant impact:

Project: Sandseel - The Exchange Market Platform

  • Leveraging the open-source Peatio framework Peatio, I worked on developing this platform.
  • Contributed to the creation of an efficient admin tool.

Project: Sandseel Util - The Platform for Deposit/Withdraw

  • Here, I delved into building Restful APIs using Golang Golang.
  • Designed and implemented workflows for depositing on-chain to off-chain and withdrawing off-chain to on-chain.
  • Developed a client SDK and conducted extensive research on the Cosmos Chain Cosmos, exploring aspects like Validators, Inter-Blockchain Communication (IBC), Remote Procedure Calls (RPC), and Hierarchical Deterministic (HD) Wallets.

My return to Tiki has reignited my passion for building innovative solutions in a dynamic and collaborative environment.
