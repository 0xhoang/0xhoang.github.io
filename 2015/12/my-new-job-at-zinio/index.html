My New Job at Zinio

Introduction

At Zinio, a digital eMagazines company, I found myself in a dynamic and diverse work environment.

Website: https://www.zinio.com

What I Did

One of the key projects I worked on during my time at Zinio was:

Project: Zenit - Digital Magazine Management Platform

  • I played a significant role in building the API for this platform, leveraging PHP and Symfony.
  • Technology used: Symfony
  • I conducted research on search engines and implemented ElasticSearch to enhance content discovery.
  • Technology used: Elastic
  • To optimize system performance, I worked on improving SQL queries and applying caching mechanisms.
  • Ensuring system reliability and minimizing downtime were among my responsibilities.
  • Rigorous testing, including unit tests and function tests, was part of my routine.
  • I also contributed to the documentation, providing clarity and guidance for the API.

My experience at Zinio introduced me to an English-speaking work environment and allowed me to collaborate with people from various countries, broadening my horizons and skills.
