Exploring the World of Incognito Chain

Introduction

Joining the dynamic world of Incognito, a blockchain platform, opened up exciting opportunities to delve into the realm of blockchain and privacy-enhancing technologies.

Website: https://incognito.org

My Role and Contributions

During my time with Incognito, I was involved in several significant projects:

Project: Incognito Chain - Platform for Privacy Blockchain

  • Made modest contributions to core Incognito Chain features

Project: Blockchain Backend - Microservices for Incognito Wallet

  • Transformed the architecture from monolithic to microservices, enhancing scalability and modularity.
  • Contributed to the development of features for Shielded and Un-Shielded services.
  • Worked on features related to Farming and Pool Provider services.
  • Developed services for managing validator nodes and trading tokens.
  • Created a Software Development Kit (SDK) for improved integration.
  • Leveraged Golang and RESTful API for seamless execution.

Project: Exploring Incognito Chain - Data Delivery and Storage

  • Designed and implemented a data pipeline utilizing OLAP, Kafka, and Golang.
  • Visualized data through the Imply platform for enhanced insights and analytics.
    • Technology used: Druid, Imply

My journey at Incognito was filled with exciting challenges and valuable contributions to the blockchain and privacy sectors.
